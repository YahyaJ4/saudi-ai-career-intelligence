# HUMAIN Company Profile

Year: 2025
Entity: PIF / HUMAIN
Category: company_profiles
Source type: web_profile
Source: https://www.pif.gov.sa/en/our-investments/our-portfolio/humain/

HUMAIN is a Public Investment Fund company launched in May 2025 to build Saudi Arabia's full AI stack, including data centers, cloud infrastructure, AI models, and applications. It is positioned as a major contributor to Vision 2030's economic diversification and knowledge-economy goals.

Career relevance:
- AI infrastructure
- Cloud platforms
- Large language models
- Arabic AI applications
- Data center operations
- Enterprise AI solutions
