# Initial Market Insights

This analysis uses the current 72 parsed job postings in `data/job_postings/processed/job_postings.csv`.

## Main Finding

The dataset suggests that the strongest market-facing positioning is not only "AI student" or "data scientist", but:

> AI/Data engineer who can build practical AI systems, analyze real data, and communicate career or business value.

## Role Demand

The top role categories are Data Science, AI Engineering, Consulting, GenAI / LLM, and Software Engineering. This supports focusing on a portfolio that blends:

- RAG and LLM systems
- data analysis and dashboarding
- production-style ML workflows
- consulting-style communication

## Skill Demand

The most frequent skills and tools include AI, Machine Learning, PyTorch, TensorFlow, Docker, SQL, Azure, AWS, MLOps, RAG, LLMs, and Power BI.

This matches Yahya's current strengths in Python, PyTorch, RAG, and applied ML, while confirming that Docker, SQL, LangChain, Power BI, and deployment are high-value skills to strengthen.

## Sector Demand

Most postings in the dataset are classified as Technology, with smaller but important signals from Fintech and Consulting. This supports building projects that feel practical and business-facing rather than purely academic.

## Portfolio Recommendation

Build three projects in this order:

1. Saudi AI Job Market Dashboard
2. Fintech RAG Analyst
3. ML Prediction API with Docker

Together, these show data analysis, RAG engineering, deployment readiness, and clear understanding of the Saudi AI job market.
