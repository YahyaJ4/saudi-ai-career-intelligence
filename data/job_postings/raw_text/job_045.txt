Confidential Government Business Analyst (AI & Data Projects) About the job About the Role: We are looking for a passionate and analytical Business Analyst to join our team at the National Center for Artificial Intelligence. In this role, you will act as a bridge between business stakeholders and technical teams, translating business needs into clear requirements for cutting-edge AI solutions. This is an exciting opportunity for a fresh graduate to launch a career in the field of Artificial Intelligence, Generative Models, and Smart Infrastructure. Key Responsibilities: Requirements Gathering: Collaborate with various departments to gather, analyze, and document business requirements for AI and smart solution projects. Strategic Alignment: Translate strategic organizational goals into clear functional and technical requirements for the development teams. Project Support: Support the planning and execution of technical projects, including Generative AI models, automation, and smart infrastructure. Collaboration: Work closely with Data Science, Engineering, and Infrastructure teams to understand technical capabilities and ensure alignment with business needs. Documentation: Create detailed use cases, workflow models, and process maps to guide project development. Reporting & Analysis: Prepare analytical reports and presentations to support executive decision-making. Performance Monitoring: Monitor the performance of AI initiatives, analyze results, and provide recommendations for continuous improvement. Market Research: Stay updated on the latest trends and applications in Artificial Intelligence and provide insights to the team. Qualifications & Requirements: Education: Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, Information Systems, Data Science, or a related field. Experience: Fresh Graduate (0-1 years of experience). Skills: Strong analytical and problem-solving skills. Ability to document complex technical requirements clearly. Excellent verbal and written communication skills. Strong interest in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science. Proficiency in Microsoft Office (Excel, PowerPoint, Word).
