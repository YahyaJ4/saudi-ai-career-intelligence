55. Confidential Data Scientist About the job The Data Scientist will lead the design, development, and implementation of advanced analytics and business intelligence solutions. This role is pivotal in driving the Group’s data strategy, building scalable data warehouse models, and delivering actionable insights using Microsoft BI tools or similar BI tools. The Data Scientist will ensure all data practices align with business objectives, governance standards, and the strategic goals. Responsibilities Deliver end-to-end BI and data warehouse solutions using Power BI, SQL Server, Azure Data Factory, and Synapse. Design and maintain scalable data warehouse and data lake architectures. Collaborate with stakeholders to define KPIs and deliver impactful dashboards. Conduct advanced data analysis and predictive modeling. Ensure data quality, governance, security, and compliance. Integrate ERP and business systems (SAP, Oracle Fusion,…). Support Agile project delivery and documentation. Champion Data Ops/DevOps best practices. Qualifications Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field. Minimum 6 years of experience in data science, analytics, or BI functions. Proven experience leading BI/data warehouse projects using Microsoft tools. Expertise in SQL, DAX, Power Query, and data modeling. Experience with Azure-based data tools. Proficiency in Arabic and English.
